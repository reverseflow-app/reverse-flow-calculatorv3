Formula

Total friction loss = PDP − nozzle pressure − appliance/elevation loss.

Friction loss per 100' = total friction loss ÷ hose length in hundreds of feet.

GPM = √(FL per 100' ÷ coefficient) × 100. Final result rounds to the nearest 5 GPM.

Default coefficients: 1.75” = 15.5, 1.88” = 8, 2.5” = 2, 3” = 0.8, 5” = 0.08.

About / How to Use

Reverse Flow Mode estimates GPM by working backward from PDP, nozzle pressure, hose length, and hose coefficient.

Required PDP Mode calculates the pump discharge pressure needed to achieve a target GPM.

For smoothbore calculations, select the tip size manually. Smoothbore nozzle pressures that cannot be reached with the entered PDP and hose setup are disabled in Reverse Flow mode.

This calculator is intended for training and reference. Always follow local pump charts, equipment testing, department policy, and instructor guidance.
